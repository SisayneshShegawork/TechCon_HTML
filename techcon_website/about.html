The goal of conference

The primary goals of a conference are to facilitate the exchange of knowledge, ideas, and experiences among individuals with shared interests or expertise. This is achieved through various activities such as presentations, discussions, workshops, and networking opportunities. The overarching aim is to promote collaboration and drive progress within specific fields or topics.
